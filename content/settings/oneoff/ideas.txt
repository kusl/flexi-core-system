One Off Ideas

Superhero Extermination Squad
    Mighty Man went off to destroy the evil korg of braxis five but has returned insane due to a virus
    that affects metahumans.  This is top secret and the government can't trust any superheroes to do
    the job without getting infected.  The players are drawn from the ranks of various governmental
    agencies to form the SES superhero extermination squad.  They must track down Mighty Man and solve
    the problem or the world is doomed.
